Work Bio

I have been a Mortgage Loan Originator since January of 2003 and have concentrated on the purchase side of the business partnering with many of the top Realtors in the area. I went to St. Viator High School in Arlington Heights and went to Northern Illinois University where I earned a Bachelor of Science Degree in Economics. Although I can do refinance as well as purchase loans, I take special pride in being well versed on the VA loan and helping Veterans get into the best loan for them and their families. Our Veterans sacrafices so much to ensure the safety of our country. I in turn learned to be a great advocate for them by educating them on what is the best mortgage for them for both short and long term from a financial stand point.
