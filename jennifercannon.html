Work Bio

I began my career in the mortgage lending industry in 1997. I have been a loan officer with CIBM Mortgage (formerly Avenue Mortgage, Inc) since 2010. I have worked with a variety of clients including first-time home buyers, those upsizing and those downsizing. I enjoy helping clients restructure their finances with a refinance. More recently, I have focused on helping clients navigate home ownership and refinance as they go through or move on from a divorce. People needing my help in this area seemed to have found me as opposed to me looking for them. I am humbled that my clients allow me to help them through such a personal and difficult time in their lives.
