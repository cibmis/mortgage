Why Choose Me?

Buying a home, especially your first, is an awesome experience and possibly the largest investment you will make. It is very important that you work with a lender that has the knowledge and experience to guide you through the process. A lot has changed in the mortgage industry over the last several years, and the lender you choose could be the difference between getting the keys to your new home or having your loan fall through.

With over 10 years of experience, you can trust that I will help you navigate through every step of the home buying process. Since everyone’s situation is different, I will help customize the right loan product to fit your needs. At CIBM Bank, we offer a full array of products including Conventional, FHA, VA, USDA Rural Housing, as well as down payment assistance programs. I’m committed to providing you the best service possible, because that would be my expectation in your shoes.

Whether you are buying, refinancing or even building your dream home, my goal is to make your experience as smooth as possible. Getting preapproved is easy! You can apply from the comfort of your home with our secure online application, by phone, or we can schedule a time to meet in person. Let me help you on this journey, and make your dream of homeownership a reality!
