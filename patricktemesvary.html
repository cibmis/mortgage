Work Bio

Patrick has originated mortgage loans since 1985. Through these years Patrick has assisted thousands of Buyers & Borrowers “turning their dreams into a home”. From Residential to Commercial properties he can handle almost any type of transaction. As a Nationwide Lender with small town service, he gets to know YOU and treats you like family not a number.

Specializing with 1st time home buyers, our clients understand the process as we hand carry them from start to finish. Customer Service is our number 1 Goal. Being a Hybrid Lender we have all the Mortgage Programs needed for all types of borrowers and transactions. Call, Click, Email and let Patrick Temesvary show you how we can help you and your family close your mortgage loan efficiently and without stress.

Patrick is the Commander of the Medinah Motor Corps - A motorcycle precision drill team and a parade unit of the Medinah Shrine Center in Addison, Illinois. Performing on White Ultra Classic-Shrine Edition Harley-Davidson Motorcycles and pleasing audiences since 1955. They have also been in commercials and movies viewed in the Chicagoland area.
