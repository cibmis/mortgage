Lot Loan

Found the perfect spot for your dream home, we can help you finance the purchase of the lot, with a CIBM Lot Loan. Loan is for 12 months and payments are interest only. Building must commence in this timeframe.

Construction Loan

We offer two options for construction loans. The most popular is our single close construction program. This provides one loan to cover both construction costs and your mortgage.

FHA 203(k) Loan

Does your dream home need some changes to make it work for you? This loan type offers financing for a new home plus the cost for eligible repairs, additions, or reconstruction. You can get up to $35,000 for renovations at the time of purchase.
